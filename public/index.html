About the Project

What is ShelfSense AI?

Revolutionary AI technology designed to solve retail's biggest challenge: maintaining optimal inventory levels across all store locations.

The Problem We Solve

Empty shelves cost retailers billions annually in lost sales and customer frustration. Traditional manual checks are time-consuming, error-prone, and can't keep pace with modern retail demands.

Our Solution

ShelfSense AI uses state-of-the-art computer vision algorithms to continuously monitor your shelves, instantly identifying products and gaps. When stock runs low, the system automatically alerts your team, enabling rapid response and ensuring shelves stay full.

This intelligent solution helps retailers maintain optimal stock levels, reduce out-of-stock situations by up to 85%, improve operational efficiency, and significantly enhance customer satisfaction.

Key Features

Powerful Capabilities

Everything you need to revolutionize your inventory management and maximize sales

Real-Time Detection

Continuously monitors shelf inventory with 99% accuracy, identifying products and empty spaces instantly with advanced computer vision technology.

Instant Alerts

Automatically generates real-time notifications when missing items are detected, enabling your team to respond immediately and minimize out-of-stock time.

Visual Annotations

Clear visual feedback with AI-generated bounding boxes around detected items, making it easy to understand exactly what's on your shelves at a glance.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard with detailed detection statistics, historical trends, and actionable insights across all aisles and store locations.
